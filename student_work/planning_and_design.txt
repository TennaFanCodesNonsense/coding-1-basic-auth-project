=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
=========================

1. PROJECT NAME & THEME
-------------------------
- What is your app called, and what type of app is it?  
Fandom World, you log in, and you look at your name and sprites/images depending on what Fandom you signed up with.
- What style or vibe do you want users to experience?
Basic, sleek.
- What about your app will require a log-in structure?
Login screen, click make an account. You make a username and password AND choose from a dropdown what fandom you are signing up to get
content from. All that is stored in a dictionary, and when you click
return to login screen, and you sign in there, you get to the main site, which now shows photos for your fandom.

2. CORE FUNCTIONALITY
-------------------------
- What are the essential features your app must have?  
Sign up screen, log in screen, welcome page, and a few subpages of photos.
- What optional features could make your app more interactive or personalized?
Says your username on the main page when you log in. **If we have time, you can make a profile picture.

3. SECRET OR PERSONALIZED CONTENT

-------------------------
- What content will users see after logging in? 
Their username in the welcome message, and 1-3 pages with fanart or sprites belonging to a specific fandom. 
- Will this content change depending on who is using the app (admin, user, special user?)
Yes. the name will change and again, what fandom content you see. If we have time we will do a admin thing. 

4. USER EXPERIENCE (UX) DESIGN
-------------------------
- How should your app look and feel? 
Generic. Fandom oriented.
- How will users navigate between pages? 
Buttons.

5. SECURITY FEATURES
-------------------------
- What password and authentication rules will you enforce?
Include special characters, and make at least 8 long. There will be a message if your password doesn't meet requirements.
- How will you protect secret pages from unauthorized access? 
You need to log in first to get to your page. If someone doesn't have your password they can't get into your specific secret page.
